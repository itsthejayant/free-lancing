About Us

At NorwayKids, we share our passion and commitment in preschool education through innovative ideas for excellence. We believe in nurturing young buds, by emphasising on individual needs, talents, abilities and interest. We believe that every child needs the best support in enhancing the potential.
NorwayKids's elementary bilingual curriculum is designed to educate the child with music, arts, science, technology, physically, intellectually, socially and emotionally.The development of a child is minutely observed and nurtured by dedicated, enthusiastic and skilled staff, whose passion is appreciated by the parents.
Our Mission: NorwayKids sets its energy in delivering positive energy to our child in a safe and secure environment. Critical thinker, society norm and well- rounded grown up who is capable of utilising technology in innovative ways. A child needs to grow and understand his/her value as an individual and develope in all areas of intelligence
Our Vission: NorwayKids's goal is dedicated in creating the leaders of the future. We are committed to build values in our young ones.
We envision our young buds as future successful icons. Our child- centric approach is our USP. The learning activities are focused on the child's creativity and talent
